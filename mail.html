Mail

All mailboxes are encrypted with a private key that is unlocked from a users email password, and as such losing access to your password will mean complete loss of access to mail, please use a password manager as administrators will be unable to assist you in recovery.

Only IMAP is available as a connection to the mail server, there are currently no plans to add POP3.
